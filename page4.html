01 研发

01 Research and Development

研发中心拥有先进检测能力的实验型动力混合机、气象色谱仪、恒温恒湿老化试验箱等先进设备，不断完善中心需求。完成了一种强力分散机的搅拌部件、一种强力分散机、用于强力分散机的料桶传输定位装置等多项实用新型发明专利，累计20多项。

Our R&D center is equipped with advanced experimental dynamic mixers, gas chromatographs, and constant temperature and humidity aging test chambers, among other advanced equipments. We have developed several utility model patents, including a stirring component for high-shear dispersers, a high-shear disperser, and a material barrel transfer and positioning device for high-shear dispersers, totaling over 20 patents.

02 技改

02 Technical Improvement

全粉体自动管道输送系统（没粉尘，投放配份比例标准化）；PLC一键启动全自动生产系统；一线品牌胶原料，环保无污染。

We are equipped with a fully automated powder pipeline conveying system that is dust-free and ensures standardized dosing ratios. We have a PLC one-touch start fully automated production system. Additionally, we use top-brand adhesive raw materials that are environmentally friendly and non-polluting.

03 生产

03 Production

采用国内目前最先进的全自动化制胶、分装生产线连续投产作业，相比传统制胶方式，效率大大提高，颗粒、一泡出现在成品率90%以上。公司实行6S管理模式，改善了车间生产环境。

We utilize the most advanced fully automated adhesive production and packaging line in the country, enabling continuous production operations. Compared to traditional methods, our efficiency is significantly improved, with a granule and foam occurrence rate of over 90% in the final product. We implement the 6S management model, which has enhanced the production environment in our workshops.

04 质检

04 Quality Inspection

公司严格按照密封胶生产工艺流程规范化操作，并建立健全完善的成品检测体系，产品生产过程到合格出厂，共计需要完成表观、表干、老化、紫外、盐浴、酸治、端变实验等最多35项检测。

We strictly adhere to standardized procedures in the sealant production process and have established a comprehensive finished product testing system. Our products undergo up to 35 rigorous tests from production to delivery, including appearance, surface drying, aging, UV exposure, salt bath, acid treatment, and end deformation tests.
