SPIRIT OF ENTERPRISE

YICHANG HAOJIAO NEW MATERIAL TECHNOLOGY CO. LTD

01 Research and Development

Our R&D center is equipped with advanced experimental dynamic mixers, gas chromatographs, and constant temperature and humidity aging test chambers, among other advanced equipments. We have developed several utility model patents, including a stirring component for high-shear dispersers, a high-shear disperser, and a material barrel transfer and positioning device for high-shear dispersers, totaling over 20 patents.

02 Technical Improvement

We are equipped with a fully automated powder pipeline conveying system that is dust-free and ensures standardized dosing ratios. We have a PLC one-touch start fully automated production system. Additionally, we use top-brand adhesive raw materials that are environmentally friendly and non-polluting.

03 Production

We utilize the most advanced fully automated adhesive production and packaging line in the country, enabling continuous production operations. Compared to traditional adhesive manufacturing methods, efficiency is significantly improved, with a reduction of over 90% in granule and bubble occurrences. The company implements the 6S management model, which has enhanced the production environment in our workshops.

04 Quality Inspection

We strictly adhere to standardized procedures in the sealant production process and have established a comprehensive finished product testing system. From production to delivery, our products undergo up to 35 rigorous tests, including appearance, surface drying, aging, UV exposure, salt bath, acid bath, and creep tests.
