Since its establishment, Yichang Haojiao New Material Technology Co., Ltd. has been dedicated to the research and development, production, and sales of single-component silicone sealants, two-component silicone sealants, polyurethane foam sealants, silane-modified sealants, tile grout, and environmentally friendly high-elastic sealants. The factory covers an area of over 50,000 square meters. We have established five major operational teams nationwide and over 200 county-level operators, with an annual production and sales volume of 300 million yuan.

宜昌皓胶现有先进的硅酮胶自动生产线10条，发泡胶生产线8条，美缝剂自动生产线4条，环保高弹胶生产线多条，所有产品均采用高品质、高标准的原材料，对生产的每个流程环节进行科学的控制和有效的监督确保生产的每一款产品都符合行业标准。

We currently operate 10 advanced automated production lines for silicone sealants, 8 production lines for foam sealants, 4 automated production lines for tile grout, and multiple production lines for environmentally friendly high-elastic sealants. All of our products are made using high-quality, high-standard raw materials. Every production process is scientifically controlled and effectively supervised to ensure that each product meets industry standards.
