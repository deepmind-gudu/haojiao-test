万胶之王-6500 590ml 中性

THE KING OF ALL GUMS - 6500 590ml Neutral

产品特性

Product Features

  • 单组份，使用方便，在4℃-40℃的温度范围内具有良好的可挤出性和触变性，用于胶枪打出直接施工即可；
  • Single-component, easy to use, with excellent extrudability and thixotropy within a temperature range of 4°C to 40°C, suitable for direct application using a caulking gun.
  • 中性固化，本品无甲醛，净味环保，防霉抗菌，在水中也可固化，对绝大多数基材无腐蚀，尤其适用于高品质家装用户使用；
  • Neutral curing, formaldehyde-free, environmentally friendly with a clean smell, mold and bacteria resistant, capable of curing in water, non-corrosive to most substrates, especially suitable for high-quality home decoration users.
  • 具有良好的粘结性，固化时与大多数建筑材料形成很强的密封而不需要使用底涂；
  • Excellent adhesion, forming a strong seal with most building materials during curing without the need for a primer.
  • 固化后在低温下不变硬，不变脆，在高温环境下会收缩，位移能力变差；
  • After curing, it does not harden or become brittle at low temperatures, but will shrink and have reduced displacement capability in high-temperature environments.
  • 与其他全能型耐候结构胶有良好的相容性。
  • Shows good compatibility with other all-purpose weather-resistant structural adhesives.

主要用途

Main Applications

  • 家装用户粘接及填缝；
  • Assembly bonding and joint filling
  • 铝塑板及小型瓷砖粘接填缝；
  • Adhesive bonding and grouting of aluminum-plastic panels and small ceramic tiles
  • 不锈钢、铁、铜等金属基材粘接填缝；
  • Seaming for metal materials such as galvanized sheets, iron plates, and aluminum plates
  • 厨卫工程装配；
  • Kitchen and bathroom assembly/installation
  • 家装门窗装配；
  • Installation of household doors and windows
  • 其他经实验适用的应用。
  • Other verified applications
