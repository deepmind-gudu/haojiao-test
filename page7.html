万胶之王-6500 590ml 中性

THE KING OF ALL GUMS - 6500 590ml Neutral

产品特性

Product Features

  • 单组份，使用方便，在4℃-40℃的温度范围内具有良好的可挤出性和触变性，用于胶枪打出直接施工即可；
  • Single-component, easy to use: Exhibits excellent extrudability and thixotropy within the temperature range of 4°C to 40°C. Can be applied directly using a caulking gun.
  • 中性固化，对金属、镀锌板和镀锌钢材无腐蚀性，应用广泛；
  • Neutral cure: Non-corrosive to metals, galvanized sheets, and galvanized steel, making it widely applicable.
  • 良好的粘结性能，与大多数建筑材料形成很强的粘结性而无需使用底涂；
  • Excellent adhesion performance: Forms strong bonds with most building materials without the need for a primer.
  • 低模量配方，伸长率高，不变形，在高温环境下不会收缩，位移能力强，不移位；
  • Low modulus formulation: High elongation rate, non-deforming, does not shrink under high-temperature conditions, strong displacement capacity, and does not shift.
  • 与多种建筑密封胶具有良好的相容性。
  • Good compatibility: Compatible with various building sealants.

主要用途

Main Applications

  • 装配用粘接及填缝；
  • Assembly bonding and joint filling
  • 门窗安装填缝密封；
  • Sealing for door and window installations
  • 镀锌板、铁板、铝板等金属材料接缝；
  • Seaming for metal materials such as galvanized sheets, iron plates, and aluminum plates
  • 屋顶工程接配；
  • Roofing joint projects
  • 地砖填缝；
  • Tile joint filling
  • 其他建筑适用的应用。
  • Other applicable construction uses
